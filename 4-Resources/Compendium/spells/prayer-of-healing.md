# Prayer of Healing
*2nd-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 10 minute
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range each regain hit points equal to `dice: 2d8|avg` (`2d8`) + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.

**Classes**: [Cleric](4-Resources/Compendium/classes/cleric.md), [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](4-Resources/Compendium/classes/sorcerer-divine-soul-xge.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 267. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*
