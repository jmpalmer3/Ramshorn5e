# Power Word Heal
*9th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A wave of healing energy washes over the creature you touch. The target regains all its hit points. If the creature is [charmed](4-Resources/Compendium/rules/conditions.md#charmed), [frightened](4-Resources/Compendium/rules/conditions.md#frightened), [paralyzed](4-Resources/Compendium/rules/conditions.md#paralyzed), or [stunned](4-Resources/Compendium/rules/conditions.md#stunned), the condition ends. If the creature is [prone](4-Resources/Compendium/rules/conditions.md#prone), it can use its reaction to stand up. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

**Classes**: [Bard](4-Resources/Compendium/classes/bard.md), [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](4-Resources/Compendium/classes/sorcerer-divine-soul-xge.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 266*
